ScrollBar

Vertical only. Three hit zones: top 16 px = up arrow, middle = track, bottom 16 px = down arrow. Always paired with a SelectBox or TextBox.

Geometry

▲
▼
scrollposition: 2 / scrollmax: 10
Up arrow: 16 px tall, click → scrollUp().
Track: drag thumb (or wheel forwarded).
Down arrow: 16 px tall, click → scrollDown().
draw = false until content overflows.
Mouse wheel events forwarded by Interface.
Keyboard nav past viewport plays whoom.wav.
