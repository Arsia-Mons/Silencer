- **Learnings:**
  - Adding bank 7 + bank 134 to a screen-builder's load list is a pure additive
    change with zero impact on prior screens — `SpriteSet::Load` is per-screen,
    so even though the OPTIONS hub doesn't load bank 7, OPTIONSCONTROLS' load of
    bank 7 doesn't perturb anything. This is the right granularity: each screen
    declares its own load list, no shared "all banks" path that grows monotonically.
  - The "minimal C0/D0 commit" pattern (only bg plate at the build/run gate)
    deliberately defers the structural composition to C1+, on the theory that
    the Frame panel is the most distinct new widget on this screen and deserves
    its own iteration to verify (codec, anchor convention, transparency mask).
    This is a small departure from the "structurally complete on first commit"
    pattern used for OPTIONS / OPTIONSAUDIO, motivated by the spec's explicit
    flag that bank 7 idx 7 may use tile-mode RLE and may be a new visual element
    not yet tested. Splitting C0/C1 isolates the bank 7 risk from the screen-switch.
