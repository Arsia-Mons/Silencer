Compete with other players in a futuristic Martian city where an oppressive government has begun to sell the air itself. Choose your side in an information war with fast-paced shoot-outs and unique game play.

Silencer is an easy to learn action/strategy multiplayer side-scrolling platform game that emphasizes team play. Players control hackers on Mars called “Silencers”, who are competing against each other to gain “secrets” by hacking military secrets. The first team to gain three “secrets” wins the mission.

— Mind Control Software, 2000
